Frequently Asked Questions

Students can join from Grade 6 onwards. Requirements include previous academic records, birth certificate, and completion of our entrance assessment. We welcome students from all backgrounds who are committed to learning.

Primary instruction is in Tamil, with English and Sinhala taught as additional languages. This approach preserves our cultural heritage while ensuring students are multilingual and globally competitive.

We offer a wide range of activities including sports (cricket, basketball, volleyball), cultural programs (music, dance, drama), academic clubs (science, literary society), and community service projects.

Parents can join our Parent-Teacher Association, volunteer for school events, participate in cultural programs, and attend regular parent meetings. We believe strong parent-school collaboration enhances student success.
