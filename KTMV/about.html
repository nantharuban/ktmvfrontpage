Our Legacy

மத்திய மாகாணத்தின் எழில் கொஞ்சும் கந்தேனுவர கிராமத்தில், அறிவொளி பரப்பும் ஆலயமாக திகழ்கிறது எமது வித்தியாலயம். Founded over 50 years ago, Kandenuwara Tamil Maha Vidyalayam has been a beacon of knowledge and culture in the Matale district. We are committed to fostering academic excellence and personal growth in a supportive and inclusive environment.

Our institution has grown from humble beginnings to become one of the most respected Tamil medium schools in the Central Province. We serve students from Grade 6 to 13, providing comprehensive education that prepares them for higher studies and future careers.

At KTMV, we emphasize holistic development through academics, sports, cultural activities, and community service. Our mission is to create responsible global citizens who are proud of their Tamil heritage while being ready to contribute to the modern world.

Our Vision

To be a leading educational institution that preserves Tamil culture and language while preparing students for global success through innovative teaching and character development.

Our Mission

To provide quality education in Tamil medium, foster critical thinking, promote cultural values, and develop well-rounded individuals who contribute positively to society.

Our Core Values

Innovation

Embracing new ideas, technologies, and teaching methods to enhance learning experiences.
